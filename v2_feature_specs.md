# VantagePoint V2: Advanced Feature Specifications
## Deep-Dive into the "Cognitive Intelligence" Era

Phase V2 expands the functional footprint of VantagePoint through automated, high-fidelity intelligence and workflow governance.

---

## 1. AI-Driven Mapping & Similarity Engine
### Core Engineering: NLP-Based Extraction
*   **Concept-to-Patent Mapping**: Beyond simple keyword matching, the engine uses vector embeddings to find patents relevant to a written drug formulation concept or chemical description.
*   **NLP Claim Extraction**: Automatically parses Patent PDFs (EPO/USPTO) to extract "Active Ingredient" claims and suggest potential API associations.
*   **Similarity Scoring Engine**: Calculates molecular and structural overlap between new drug registry entries and existing patent portfolios to flag infringement risks early.

## 2. Interactive Global 3D Mapping
### 2. Strategic Strategy & Expiry Simulator
*   **"What-If" Expiry Analysis**: Interactive timeline where users can simulate the impact of Supplementary Protection Certificates (SPCs) and Patent Term Extensions (PTEs).
*   **Market Entry Modeling**: Forecast generic entry dates across different jurisdictions based on data exclusivity and patent expiry simulations.
*   **Commercial Gap Detection**: Visual identification of "white space" in the global patent mesh where new filings could provide strategic protection.

## 3. GxP Compliance & Quality Lifecycle
### 3. GxP Collaborative Governance & Redlining
*   **Immutable Peer Review**: A document viewer for patent drafts and SOPs that tracks every comment, highlight, and edit in the 21 CFR Part 11 audit trail.
*   **Stage-Gate Approval Engine**: Customizable multi-step approval paths (e.g., Scientist -> IP Counsel -> QA Director) with mandatory re-authentication "electronic signatures" at every gate.
*   **Automated Audit Pulse**: One-click generation of GxP-validated compliance reports summarizing all record changes over a specified period.

## 4. Global Market Heatmaps
### Core Engineering: Jurisdictional Intelligence
*   **Coverage Map**: A real-time, interactive global heatmap showing patent coverage by country.
*   **Risk Overlays**: Visualization of market entries and potential generic threats on a geographic scale.
*   **Rule Engine Dashboard**: Visual interface for managing dynamic jurisdictional laws without code changes.

## 5. Enterprise Interop & Collaboration
## 5. Custom Enterprise Report Bureau
*   **Drag-and-Drop Builder**: Interface for creating custom, brandable PDF and PowerPoint reports for boardroom presentations and executive summaries.
*   **Integrative BI Connectors**: Secure endpoints for external tools (Tableau, PowerBI) to ingest VantagePoint intelligence for broader corporate strategy.
*   **Scheduled Delivery**: Automated generation and email distribution of "Patent Expiry Watchlists" to key stakeholders.

## 6. Omni-Channel Ingestion & Multi-Output Tracking
### Core Engineering: Data Normalization Pipeline
*   **Tiered Input Ingestion**: 
    *   **Staff Input**: Manual record creation via GxP-validated web forms.
    *   **Bulk Migration**: Secure ingestion of legacy datasets via CSV/Excel mapping tools.
    *   **Live Sync**: Automated daily updates from global patent offices (WIPO/EPO/USPTO).
    *   **Cognitive OCR**: AI extraction of structured metadata from raw scientific PDF documents.
*   **Multi-Outcome Intelligence Tracking**:
    *   Every data input (Input) is automatically tracked across multiple critical outcomes (Outputs):
    *   **Roadmap Output**: Adjusting the Strategic Expiry Timeline.
    *   **Heatmap Output**: Updating the Global Risk Visualization.
    *   **Audit Output**: Creating a non-repudiable log in the 21 CFR Part 11 Sentinel.
    *   **Compliance Output**: Updating the GxP Validation Matrix for all active assets.

## 💡 Engineering Principles for V2
> [!NOTE]
> All AI-driven features will follow the **"Human-in-the-Loop"** principle, where system suggestions must be formally acknowledged and signed by a human role (Legal/R&D) to preserve GxP accountability.
