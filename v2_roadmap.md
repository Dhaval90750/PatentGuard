The transition to **VantagePoint V2** transforms the platform from a reactive tracking tool into a proactive, intelligent **Decision-Support Ecosystem**. Phase V2 is centered on **Functional Excellence**, **Predictive Strategy**, and **Immersive Regulatory Governance**.

---

## 🎯 Global V2 Objectives

1.  **AI-Driven Semantic Discovery**: Beyond keywords—find patents relevant to complex scientific concepts and formulation descriptions.
2.  **Strategic Strategy Simulation**: A "What-If" engine to visualize the impact of legal challenges, SPC/PTE extensions, and market entry scenarios.
3.  **Omni-Channel Data Ingestion Engine**: High-fidelity ingestion from multiple points: Manual Entry, WIPO/EPO/USPTO Live APIs, and Bulk Document OCR.
4.  **Multi-Outcome Tracking Intelligence**: Automated tracking across various output points: Expiry Timelines, Global Risk Heatmaps, and GxP Compliance Reports.
5.  **Relational Enterprise Engine**: Transition to a high-concurrency Relational Database (PostgreSQL) with integrated data normalization for all input sources.

## 🗓️ 4-Phase V2 Rollout Plan

### Phase 1: The Core Stabilization (Infrastructure)
*   **Database Migration**: Standardize on PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM.
*   **RBAC 2.0**: Enhanced granular permissions for multi-tenant enterprise environments.
*   **API Performance**: Optimize the centralized networking layer for high-volume data retrieval.

### Phase 2: The Intelligence & Simulation Layer (Strategic Design)
*   **"What-If" Strategy Simulator**: Interactive timeline to model patent extensions and commercial protection windows.
*   **Concept-Based Search**: LLM-driven discovery for finding overlapping patent claims using natural language scientific descriptions.
*   **Risk Heatmap 2.0**: Immersive global map overlay showing where IP exclusivity is strongest vs. most vulnerable based on jurisdictional law.

### Phase 3: The Collaborative Governance Layer (GxP Immersive)
*   **Audit-Ready Redlining**: Collaborative document viewer with real-time commenting and 21 CFR Part 11 "Track Changes."
*   **Stage-Gate Workflow Engine**: Customizable sequential approval paths (Scientist -> Legal -> QA) with mandatory eSign at every gate.
*   **3D Relationship Atlas**: WebGL-powered interactive mapping of the Drug-API-Patent "Web."

### Phase 4: The Ingestion & Output Bureau (Scale)
*   **Omni-Channel Data Pipeline**: Normalizing inputs from WIPO/EPO live feeds, CSV bulk uploads, and manual staff entries into the core GxP ledger.
*   **Multi-Outcome Tracking Engine**: Real-time cross-referencing of all inputs to update global risk heatmaps, expiry timelines, and strategic simulations.
*   **Custom Report Bureau**: Drag-and-drop builder for GxP-validated PDFs, PowerPoints, and Executive Portfolio Summaries based on tracked metrics.
*   **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: Azure AD, Okta, and SAML 2.0 support for enterprise authentication.
